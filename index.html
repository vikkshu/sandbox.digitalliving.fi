Components of the sandbox

All these features are currently available for our sandbox Nexus to try out. The sandbox is strictly for non-production use, intended as a playground for trying out Nexus capabilities and to better understand what a Nexus offers.

API Docs

All the docs you could possibly want, easily available at docs.sandbox.digitalliving.fi

Product Gateway

Product Gateway to integrate your applications to is running at product-gateway.sandbox.digitalliving.fi and is ready to serve Data Products configured in the Developer Portal for you.

Consent

Our offering also offers a solution for consent management. You can integrate your applications to it using our Consent Protocol by using the endpoint consent.sandbox.digitalliving.fi

Login portal

We support authentication via OpenID Connect, and are working together with SisuID to provide strong authentication capabilities to Nexuses. You can integrate your applications to login.sandbox.digitalliving.fi

Developer portal

Manage your data sources and applications in our Developer Portal at developer.sandbox.digitalliving.fi

Data Standards

Manage your data sources and applications in our Developer Portal. View and contribute to the current standards at github.com/digitalliving/standards or view the API definitions for the standards in web format at docs.sandbox.digitalliving.fi

... and you can get all of them to your Nexus as well!

You can also have a look how components work together in a demo application called MyCompany. It uses Login Portal to authorize a user, Product Gateway to retrieve data products based on Data Standards, and Consent provider to grant access to this data.
